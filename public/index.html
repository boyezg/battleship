Battleship Game Guide

Overview:

Battleship is a two-player strategy game. Players take turns guessing the location of each other's ships on a 10x10 grid. The objective is to sink all of your opponent's ships before they sink yours.

How to Play:

  1. Place your fleet (Aircraft Carrier, Battleship, Cruiser, Submarine, Destroyer) on your grid.
  2. Take turns guessing coordinates (e.g., "B5").
  3. "Hit" or "Miss" will be announced based on the guess.
  4. Track progress to sink all ships.

Winning:

The first player to sink all ships wins!

Tip: Spread your ships apart and watch patterns!
